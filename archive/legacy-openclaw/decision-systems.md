# Decision and Experiment System

Status: reusable historical framework.

## Decision record

For consequential choices, capture:

- Decision and timestamp
- Objective
- Alternatives considered
- Evidence and assumptions
- Reversibility and rollback path
- Dependencies and permissions
- Expected outcome
- Review date and actual outcome

## Experiment record

- Hypothesis
- Smallest safe test
- Success and failure criteria
- Start and review timestamps
- Result: won, lost, or inconclusive
- Lesson and next action

## Review rules

- Do not promote a rule from one result.
- Prefer outcome quality over activity volume.
- Recheck stale assumptions before acting.
- If execution becomes repetitive without improving the outcome, narrow scope,
  summarize state, and restart from the last verified decision.
