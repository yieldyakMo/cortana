# Historical Social Operations

Status: reusable procedure with current safety overrides.

## Useful workflow

1. Scan for high-value conversations and direct replies.
2. Select a small number of worthwhile engagement targets.
3. Draft one core message and platform-specific adaptations.
4. Review for accuracy, voice, privacy, and reputational risk.
5. Obtain Joseph's approval before any external action.
6. Log approved actions and later outcomes.
7. Review weekly for signal quality and lessons.

## Voice

- Concise, specific, strategic, and evidence-oriented
- No generic praise, spam, artificial urgency, or impersonated experience
- Prefer useful questions, concrete frameworks, and honest uncertainty

## Current safety override

The legacy policy allowed some automatic replies, follows, and likes. That
permission is revoked. Posts, replies, reactions, follows, DMs, bans, and other
external actions require explicit approval.

## Historical cadence

Old numerical posting limits and schedules are reference-only. They must be
revalidated against current platform rules, account condition, and Joseph's
goals before use.
